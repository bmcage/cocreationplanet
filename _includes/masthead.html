We developed with teachers a toolkit based on the planet methaphor. It explains the design thinking methodology and how the ExtenDT2 tools can be used during the design thinking stages

This tool guides teachers through the different phases of the development of a digital based design thinking. For every phase, i.e. continent in the planet, online tools are provided to help teachers through the process.
